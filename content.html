Our Company History
RBII Services was established in 1989 by R. J. Jeff in Mississauga, Ontario, Canada. He built the company on safety, strong customer relationships and the pillars of quality, capacity, and reliability. This approach has made RBII Services a leader in Custom Sheet Metal Fabrication, piping and pipe welding, HVAC, Mechanical Insulating and Engineered Scaffolding. Our 7,400 sq. foot shop in Mississauga allows us to deliver a wide range of custom fabrications and we pride ourselves on the ability to deliver a complete range of services tailored to meet a broad spectrum of project needs and requirements.

Our Strengths

Our skilled workforce, work ethic, efficient systems, high level of customer service and focus on workplace safety have made RBII Services contractor of choice in Canada.  We continue to strive for improvements daily in every level of our operations.
